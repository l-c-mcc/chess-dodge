Chess Dodge

Controls: W to move up, A to move left, S to move down, D to move right

Goal: Navigate your rook away from getting captured by the other pieces for as long as possible. They will capture you if you land on the same tile as them or if they land on you. Opponent pieces move within their normal chess movement abilities, but always in a downward direction.

Your piece has been captured? Refresh the page to try again.
